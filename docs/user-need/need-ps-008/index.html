Monitor major retail and service development outcomes

Partially satisfied

As a town centre or economic regeneration lead, I need to understand the volume and outcomes of major retail and service development applications so that I can monitor how town centres, high streets and service hubs are changing, and respond to shifts in commercial demand and consumer behaviour.

Justification

Justification just-0052

The `development-scale` field records the local planning authority's classification of a full or outline planning application by development scale. The `major` value lets users identify applications classified by the local planning authority as major development. Together with `application-types` and `decision-notice.decision`, this helps users segment decision outcomes for major development. These needs are only partially satisfied because major scale and decision outcome do not by themselves provide the full development-type, use, floorspace, housing, employment, industrial, warehousing, retail or service detail needed for those reporting and monitoring questions.
