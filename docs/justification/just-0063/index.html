Justification just-0063

Needs
dd-need-097
Satisfaction
full
Confidence
medium
Status
proposed

submitted-data-uri identifies the authoritative structured application data as first received by the planning authority.

Linking it to the planning application allows authorised users and systems to recover the original submitted information for later procedural, appeal or enforcement questions without prescribing how the artefact is stored.
