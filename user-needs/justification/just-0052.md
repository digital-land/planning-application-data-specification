The `development-scale` field records the local planning authority's classification of a full or outline planning application as major or minor development.

Together with `application-types` and `decision-notice.decision`, this helps users segment decision outcomes by major and minor development scale. This partially supports PS1 and PS2 needs around major employment development, uncategorised major development, uncategorised minor development and determination-time monitoring.

These needs are only partially satisfied because development scale and decision outcome do not by themselves provide the full development-type, use, floorspace, housing, employment or timing detail needed for those reporting and monitoring questions.
