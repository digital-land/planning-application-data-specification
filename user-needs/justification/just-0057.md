The `minor` value in `development-scale` lets users identify applications classified by the local planning authority as minor development.

Together with `application-types` and `decision-notice.decision`, this helps users segment decision outcomes for minor development. This partially supports the need to record uncategorised minor development proposals.

The need is only partially satisfied because development scale and decision outcome do not by themselves provide the more specific proposal category needed to know whether a minor development is uncategorised.
