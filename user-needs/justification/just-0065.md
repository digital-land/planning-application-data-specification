The `resolution-to-grant` value lets the planning permission timeline record when the determining authority resolved to grant permission before the formal decision notice was issued.

Recording the resolution to grant with `event-date` and `planning-application` lets users see that the application has moved beyond ordinary assessment and is awaiting legal agreement, call-in consideration or other finalisation.

This event is not the final legal decision. The formal decision remains recorded through the decision notice.
