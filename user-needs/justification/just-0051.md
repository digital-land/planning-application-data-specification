The `development-scale` field records the local planning authority's classification of a full or outline planning application by development scale.

For `need-ps-005`, the `major` value identifies major development applications and `decision-notice.decision` records the outcome. Linking the decision notice back to the planning application lets analysts count the volume and outcomes of major development applications.
