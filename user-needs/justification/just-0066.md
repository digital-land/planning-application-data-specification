The `resolution-to-refuse` value lets the planning permission timeline record when the determining authority resolved to refuse permission before the formal decision notice was issued.

Recording the resolution to refuse with `event-date` and `planning-application` lets users see the expected direction of the case while refusal reasons are finalised or another body considers whether to intervene.

This event is not the final legal decision. The formal decision remains recorded through the decision notice.
