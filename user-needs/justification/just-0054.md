The `hh` value in `application-types` identifies householder planning applications.

Linking a decision notice to the planning application and recording `decision` and `decision-date` lets users see the outcome of householder applications and when they were decided. Together, these fields support tracking householder proposals and decisions for casework and monitoring incremental neighbourhood change.
