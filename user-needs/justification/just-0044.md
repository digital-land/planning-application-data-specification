The `call-in-direction-issued` value lets the planning permission timeline record when a direction to call in an application was issued.

Recording the call-in event with `event-date` and `planning-application` lets users compare the call-in point with committee, consultation and decision dates so they can understand the change in route and timetable.

This only partially satisfies the need because it records that a call-in direction was issued, but does not identify whether the direction was issued by the Mayor, Secretary of State or another decision-maker.
