The `valid-from` timeline event records when the authority treated an application as valid for statutory or process purposes. The `decision-date` field records when the decision was made.

Together with `application-types` and `development-scale`, these fields let users compare validation and decision dates across broad application routes and major or minor development scale.

This partially supports determination-time monitoring. It is not marked as full because the model does not yet provide the full development-type reporting categories needed to monitor determination times across all PS1 and PS2 development types.
