name: Know when an authority has resolved to refuse
statement: >
  As a member of the public, applicant, agent or planning authority, I need to know when the determining authority has resolved to refuse permission before the formal decision notice is issued so that I can understand the expected direction of the case, even where refusal reasons are still being finalised or another body may still intervene.
actors:

scope: in
themes:
  - processing
  - transparency
source:
  - type: interview
    notes: Camden explained that a resolution to refuse can occur where a committee overturns an officer recommendation, refusal reasons still need to be formulated or agreed, or another body is considering whether to call in the application.
variations:
next_step:
notes: |
  This need is about the interim position after a determining authority has resolved to refuse permission, but before the formal decision notice is issued. It is separate from the final decision date.

  Related needs: dd-need-014, dd-need-059, dd-need-079.
