Planning permission specification viewer

This site is a simple viewer for the planning application data specifications. It lets you explore what data is required to submit an application and what data is needed at the decision stage.

Use it to browse the datasets, see the fields they contain, and check how they meet user needs. This is a proof of concept and may change as the specifications evolve.
